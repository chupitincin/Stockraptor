Methodology
What other screeners skip.
And why it matters.

Most screeners give you raw numbers and leave the analysis to you. StockRaptor combines signals that individually are weak but together are meaningful — and weights them transparently.

Sector-Adjusted Fundamentals
P/E, P/B, ROE and revenue growth benchmarked against each company's own sector. Biotech gets different yardsticks than banking.

SEC Insider Tracking

Every week StockRaptor surfaces the small caps where the data builds a coherent medium-term case — solid fundamentals, insider conviction, earnings momentum, and analyst upside all pointing the same direction.

Transparent methodology
Systematic. Repeatable.
Designed for medium-term conviction.
STEP 01
Full universe scanned daily
Every trading day StockRaptor fetches fresh data on all 1,647 small caps from FMP, SEC EDGAR and Yahoo Finance. The universe is always complete — no sampling, no stale data.
STEP 02
14 factors, weighted for medium-term validity
Fundamentals, insider activity, earnings streaks and analyst consensus carry the most weight — signals with academic evidence behind them at 3–12 month horizons. Volume and momentum are secondary signals. Each factor normalized independently so no single outlier dominates.
STEP 03
Confluence filter, then a written investment thesis
Only stocks where 3 or more independent signals align make the weekly shortlist. Each pick gets a written thesis from Claude AI — the fundamental case, what insiders are doing, and the growth estimate. Entry price stored, track record measured at 30/60/90 days vs Russell 2000.
Why StockRaptor
Not a trading alert service.
A research filter for real investors.

The signals StockRaptor uses have academic evidence behind them at 3–12 month horizons. Here's what that means in practice.

WHAT MOST SCREENERS DO
Filter by one or two raw metrics (P/E, volume) and show you a list
No sector-relative benchmarking — biotech and banking scored the same way
No insider data — or delayed, aggregated, not from primary source
No 8-K event detection — you miss FDA decisions, acquisitions, guidance changes
No measurable track record — no way to know if the signals actually work
WHAT STOCKRAPTOR DOES DIFFERENTLY
14 factors combined into one score — a single outlier can't dominate
Sector-adjusted benchmarks — each company compared to its own peer group
SEC Form 4 parsed directly from EDGAR with exact transaction dates and values
8-K material events detected within hours of filing — earnings, FDA, acquisitions
Every pick stored with entry price — measurable 1D, 5D and 1W track record
