What We Use

StockRaptor uses browser storage technologies (localStorage and sessionStorage) and cookies to provide essential platform functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Essential Storage

The following data is stored locally in your browser and is essential for the platform to function:

  • Authentication session — Stores your login session so you remain logged in between visits. Set by Supabase. Expires at session end or after 7 days.
  • User preferences — Stores your scanner settings, language preference, and last-used filters. Stored in localStorage, persists until cleared.
  • Watchlist cache — A local copy of your watchlist for faster loading. Synced with our database. Cleared on logout.
  • Plan and username — Stores your subscription plan and display name to avoid repeated server requests. Cleared on logout.

Stripe Cookies

If you subscribe to a paid plan, Stripe (our payment processor) may set cookies for fraud prevention and payment security purposes. These are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

No Analytics or Advertising Cookies

You can clear all locally stored data by logging out of StockRaptor and clearing your browser's localStorage and cookies. Note that clearing essential storage will log you out and reset your preferences.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies and storage through their settings. Disabling essential storage may prevent the platform from functioning correctly.
